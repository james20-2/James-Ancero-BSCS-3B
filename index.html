About Libtong, Naval, Biliran

Libtong is a beautiful barangay (village) located in Naval, Biliran. Known for its scenic landscapes, rich agricultural heritage, and strong sense of community, Libtong thrives on farming activities that provide livelihood for its residents. From rice, banana, and coconut farming to animal husbandry, the community is heavily reliant on these traditional industries.

The people of Libtong have a deep connection to the land, and their hard work sustains not only their families but also contributes to the broader economy of Naval and the Biliran province. The harmony between nature and farming practices in Libtong offers both beauty and livelihood.

Rice Farming

Rice farming is one of the most important agricultural jobs in our community. Rice is a staple food and a source of income for many farmers here.

Coconut Farming

Coconut farming provides both food and raw materials for various industries. Coconut trees are essential to our local economy, providing not just food but also materials for products like coconut oil.

Banana Farming

Banana farming is another significant agricultural activity. Bananas are widely consumed both locally and internationally. Farmers use various techniques to ensure a healthy harvest year-round.

Animal Farming

Animal farming, including raising livestock such as cows, goats, and chickens, is another key industry that supports our community. It provides food, leather, and other essential products.
